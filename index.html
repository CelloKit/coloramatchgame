Puzzling challenges for children

Improve your child's hand-eye coordination with our first level. The display on the right will show a color. You must match that color's button with the slot that lights up.


Level 2

The display on the left will show a number. Each button on the ColorMatch is assigned a button, in the order they appear from the left. The display will flash a sequence of numbers. You must press the buttons in the right order.ㅤㅤㅤㅤ

Level 3

This level utilizes the top display. The top display will show text. This text is colored. You must click the button that has the color of the text. So, if the text BLUE is colored green, you must click the blue button.
